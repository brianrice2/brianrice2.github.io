## Training Days

Generally 45s - 1min rest between sets and 5min rest between exercises (and halfway through pullups)

### Pullups

10x10

- Normal
- Wide
- Narrow (hands touching)
- Uneven (band) right (basically assisted one-arm pullups - a rope or towel is easier)
- Uneven (band) left
- Slow (these will be hard - just go to fail)
- Explosive

Fill to 10 sets

Use your back muscles! Chest to bar & look up

### Pushups

4x10

- Normal
- Wide
- Offset left
- Offset right

### Situps

4x20-30

- Normal
- Twist
- Flutter kicks
- Toe touch

Don't release tension at the bottom of the toe touches

### Leg Raises

3 sets

- Same side foot
- Cross foot
- Feet together

2x the list above = 1 set

Keep your back and shoulders engaged
Touch toes at the same level as your hands
Campus board preferred over bar

### Campus Board (once a week)

- Lock-off touches left
- Lock-off touches right
- Rapid fire (up & down fast)
- 2x2s
