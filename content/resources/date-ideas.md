title = "Free or Cheap Date/Activity Ideas"
+++

1. Get on the brown line and ride in the back of the train as it drives through the loop
1. Sunset walk around Lincoln Park or the lakefront
1. Tour the Lincoln Park Conservatory
1. Tour the Garfield Park Conservatory
1. Bike ride to the ice cream shop
1. Make ice cream at home in a blender
1. Make a pasta dish from scratch-noodles, sauce, everything
1. Make pizzas from scratch (and compete on toppings)
1. Make bagels from scratch (and compete on toppings)
1. Pick a random color on the color wheel. Go for a walk, taking photos that highlight that color, and compare your shots at the end.
1. Make flower bouquets for each other out of colored construction paper or pipe cleaners
1. Go dumpster diving
1. Learn a dance off of youtube
1. Watch a movie and then discuss during over a walk
1. Picnic at the lake
1. Go swing dancing
1. Play games at the local arcade / dive bar
1. Throw the frisbee at the lakefront (if it's too busy, fly a kite)
1. Get some watercolors at the art store and paint a local nature scene or each other
1. Go to the library or used book store and pick books for each other
1. Go to the thrift store and find a jigsaw puzzle or board game to play
1. Play trivia at the bar (Carol's on Wednesdays at 8pm)
1. Make some cocktails or mocktails and go for a walk
1. Go to the coffee shop, grab a nice drink, and yap or read or work together
1. Host a book club
1. Go to the gym together
1. Visit all the vintage/antique/thrift stores in a neighborhood
1. Have some tea
1. Walk around a farmer's market
1. Go buy some flowers
