### All-purpose cleaner

- 1 part water
- 1 part vinegar
- Optional: Soak lemon/orange peels for a few weeks

### Laundry detergent

- 2 parts washing soda
- 2 parts soap flakes
- 1 part borax
- Mix and use 1 Tbsp per load

### Stain remover

- 1 part castile soap or dish soap
- 2 parts hydrogen peroxide
- Spray onto solution
- Gently work into the fabric with fingers or brush
- Sit 10-15 minutes
- Wash
