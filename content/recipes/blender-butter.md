## Ingredients

- 2 cups heavy whipping cream
- 1/4 tsp salt, or to taste

## Preparation

Blend the cream only for about a minute, starting at the slowest speed and gradually increasing. Use the tamper to push into the blades.

Drain in a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth and transfer to a bowl. Work in the salt with a spatula, removing as much liquid as possible.

Transfer to parchment paper for refrigeration or freezing.

## Variations

Fold in:

- Herbs: rosemary, basil, chives, coriander, dill, sage, lemongrass, lemon thyme, lavender, vanilla, ...
- Sweeteners: honey, molasses, maple syrup, figs, prunes, raisins, dates, dried pineapple, mango, apricot, ...
- Spices: cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice, garam masala, garlic, ...
- Vegetables: jalapeño!, chile peppers, ...
