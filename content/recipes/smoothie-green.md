## Brooke Goldner Daily Drinker

- 75% of the blender packed kale
- Up to 1/2 cup flaxseeds
- 1 ripe banana & frozen mango the rest of the way (or blueberries, pineapple, ...)
- Water up to the top of the kale

Blend for 2-3 minutes!!

## Super Green

- 2 cups packed fresh baby spinach
- 1 large apple, cored
- 1 cup diced pineapple
- 1/2 ripe Hass avocado, peeled and pitted
- 1/4 cup packed fresh mint leaves
- 3 soft Medjool dates, pitted
- 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
- 2 tsp blended peeled lemon or lime
- 1 Tbsp ground flaxseeds
- Ice cubes

Blend until completely smooth, then add 2/3 cup water and blend again.

## V12 Veggie Blast

- 2 cups water
- 2 cups spinach, red kale, or other dark greens
- 1 or 2 plum tomatoes
- 1 celery rib, coarsely chopped
- 1/2 red bell pepper, quartered
- 1 small garlic clove (or 1 Tbsp chopped red onion)
- 1/2 jalapeño pepper, seeded (optional)
- 2 tsp blended peeled lemon
- 1 apple, cored and quartered
- 2 tsp chlorella (optional)
- 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
- 1/2 cup ice cubes
