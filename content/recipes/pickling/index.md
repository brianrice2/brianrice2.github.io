## Pickled Ginger (Gari)

- 8 oz fresh ginger root, peeled
- 1 1/2 tsp sea salt
- 1 cup rice vinegar
- 1/3 cup white sugar

Cut the ginger into chunks. Place in a bowl, sprinkle with salt, and stir to coat. Let sit for 30 minutes, then transfer to a mason jar.

Stir together the rice vinegar and sugar in a saucepan until the sugar is dissolved. Bring to a boil, then pour over the ginger.

Let it cool, then seal the jar and store in the fridge for at least a week. If the liquid becomes slightly pink, it's okay, don't worry about it.

Cut into thin slices for serving.
