## Ingredients

- 1 cup soy milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 lb (450g) frozen unsweetened fruit, e.g. peach slices
- 1/4 cup (50g) sugar

## Preparation

Let frozen fruits thaw for 10-15 minutes.

Add everything to the blender in the order listed. Starting at the lowest speed and slowly increasing to the highest, use the tamper to press the ingredients into the blades. Blend for ~1 minute.

## Variations

Just shoot for a ratio of 1 lb frozen fruit to 1 cup liquid:

- Use any fruit: blueberries, blackberries, peach/mango, ...
- Use other liquids: water, juice, milk, yogurt, ...
