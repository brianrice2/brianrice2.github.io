## Miso sauce/marinade

For 2 blocks tofu, or 1 block + salad:

- 2 Tbsp brown sugar
- 2 Tbsp white miso paste
- 2 Tbsp soy sauce
- 2 Tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 Tbsp oil
- Dash ground cayenne

## Goddess dressing

- 1/2 cup tahini
- 3 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 2 Tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tsp dried parsley
- 2 Tbsp avocado oil
- Juice of 1 medium lemon
- 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 Tbsp water
