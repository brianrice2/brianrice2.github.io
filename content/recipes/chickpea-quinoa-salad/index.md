## Ingredients

- 1 lb dry chickpeas
- 1 lb dry quinoa
- 1-2 cucumbers, diced
- 2 bell peppers, diced
- 1 red onion, diced

Dressing:

- 1/3 cup olive oil
- 1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
- Garlic powder, salt, pepper to taste

## Preparation

Cook the chickpeas and quinoa and refrigerate. Once cool, add the vegetables and dressing and mix.
