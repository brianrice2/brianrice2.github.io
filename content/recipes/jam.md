---
title: Refrigerator Jam
date: 2025-11-15
---

As simple as it gets.

Total time: ~30 minutes

## Ingredients

- 1 pound fruit, chopped
- 1/2 to 2/3 cup (100-130 g) granulated sugar
- 3 Tbsp lemon juice

## Preparation

Place a plate in the freezer for testing the doneness later.

Combine the ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat and mash with a fork or potato masher.

Stirring frequently, reduce until thickened, about 15-20 minutes.
