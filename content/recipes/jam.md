title = "Refrigerator Jam"
+++

## Ingredients

- 1 pound fruit, roughly chopped
- 1/2 to 2/3 cup (100-130 g) granulated sugar
- 3 Tbsp lemon juice (approx. 1 Aldi seedless lemon)

## Preparation

1. Place a plate in the freezer for testing the doneness later
2. Combine the ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat and mash with a fork or potato masher (alternatively, finely chop the fruit using a blender or food processor)
3. Reduce until thickened, about 20 minutes - stir frequently and lower the heat as needed to prevent boiling over
4. To check for doneness, drop a small amount of jam onto a small frozen plate. Let cool for a minute, then run a spoon or finger through the jam. If you can draw a line with clean edges that don’t drip, then it's ready. If it runs back together, then cook for a few more minutes and try again.
