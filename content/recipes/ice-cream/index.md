## No-Churn Ice Cream

- 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
- 1 pint heavy whipping cream
- Mix-ins, e.g. crushed oreos

Hand-whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Fold in the condensed milk with a spatula. Mix in the mix-ins.

Pour into a 5x9 pan and freeze overnight.

## Tahini Nice Cream

- 1-2 bananas
- 1 Tbsp tahini
- 1 tsp cocoa powder
- Spices: Cinnamon, chili, etc

Mash with a fork.
