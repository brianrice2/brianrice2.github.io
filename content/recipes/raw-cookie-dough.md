## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb cashews
- 2 oz rolled oats
- Cinnamon and salt to taste

- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 2.5oz maple syrup
- 2.5oz chocolate chips

## Preparation

Blend or process everything except the liquids and chocolate chips and until finely chopped.

Add in maple syrup and vanilla extract and process until sticky. Finally, blend or stir in the chocolate chips.
