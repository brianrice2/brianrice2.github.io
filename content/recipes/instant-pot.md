title = "Instant Pot Cooking Times"
+++

~3/4 cup dry per serving

## White Rice

- 1 cup dry rice : 1 cup water
- 3 min

## Brown Rice

- 1 cup dry rice : 1 cup water
- 15 min

## Black Beans

- 1 cup dry beans : 2.5 cups water (≈2 cans)
- Soaked (8+ hr): 8-10 min
- Unsoaked: 30 min

## Chickpeas

- 1 cup dry beans : 2 cups water
- Soaked (8+ hr): 15 min
- Unsoaked: 40 min

## Lentils

- 1 cup dry : 3 cups water
- 7-10 minutes

## Quinoa (Stove)

- 1 cup quinoa : 2 cups water
- Drizzle olive oil
- Bring to a rolling boil, cover and simmer 15 min, rest 10 min

Rinsing first helps remove some bitterness
