Some meal options, mostly for myself, that try to be whole-food, plant-based, low-sodium a la [How Not to Die](https://nutritionfacts.org/book/how-not-to-die/)

## Breakfast

#### Loaded Oatmeal

- 1 cup dry oats, cooked
- 2 Tbsp ground flaxseed
- 1 handful walnuts
- 1 handful blueberries or other berries
- 1 banana, sliced, if not using blueberries above
- Spices to taste: cinnamon, cloves, ...

## Dinner

#### Simple Rice and Beans

- 3/4 cup dry brown rice, cooked
- 1 can beans (1 cup dry)
- 1 onion, sliced or chopped
- 1 bell pepper or loss-leader vegetable, sliced or chopped
- A few cloves of garlic
- Spices to taste: ground black pepper, cumin, paprika, garam masala, ginger, ...

To prep:

- 2 onions
- 2-3 bell peppers
- Garlic
- 4.5 cups (2 lb) dry beans
- 12 cups water

Sautee everything besides the beans and water for a few minutes, then add the beans and water. Cook in the pressure cooker according to whatever beans you're using.
