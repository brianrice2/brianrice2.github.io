#### Simple Rice and Beans

- 3/4 cup dry brown rice, cooked
- 1 can beans (1 cup dry)
- 1 onion, sliced or chopped
- 1 bell pepper or loss-leader vegetable, sliced or chopped
- A few cloves of garlic

- 2 onions

#### Mujadara

- 2 cups green or brown lentils (dry)
- 1.5 cups long-grain white rice (dry)
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 pieces large yellow onions, thinly sliced
- 1 teaspoons cumin
- 1 teaspoons cinnamon
- 0.5 teaspoons allspice
- 6 cups water or vegetable broth
- 1.5 teaspoons salt
- 1 pieces lemon, juiced
- 1 cups plain Greek yogurt or tahini (to serve)

Caramelize onions (don't rush this): Heat 4 tablespoons olive oil in a large pot over medium-low heat. Add 3 pieces large yellow onions, thinly sliced with a pinch of salt and cook slowly, stirring occasionally, until deeply golden-brown and jammy. This takes patience.

Reserve topping onions: Scoop out about half the onions and set aside for topping. Add 1 teaspoons cumin, 1 teaspoons cinnamon, and 0.5 teaspoons allspice to the pot and stir for 30 seconds.

Cook lentils: Add 2 cups green or brown lentils (dry) and 6 cups water or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer, cover, and cook for 10 minutes

Add rice & finish: Stir in 1.5 cups long-grain white rice (dry) and 1.5 teaspoons salt, re-cover, and cook until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.

Serve: Squeeze in 1 pieces lemon, juiced and fluff with a fork. Serve topped with the reserved caramelized onions and 1 cups plain Greek yogurt or tahini (to serve).

#### Quinoa & Black Bean Power Bowl

- 2.5 cups quinoa (dry)
- 3 pieces cans black beans (15 oz each), drained & rinsed
- 2 cups frozen corn
- 1 pieces can diced tomatoes, drained
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 pieces garlic cloves, minced
- 2 teaspoons cumin
- 1 teaspoons chili powder
- 2 pieces limes, juiced
- 1 teaspoons salt
- 1 cups shredded cheddar or crumbled feta (optional)

Cook quinoa: Rinse 2.5 cups quinoa (dry) under cold water. Combine with 5 cups water and a pinch of salt in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, reduce to low, cover, and cook until water is absorbed and tails appear.

Toast beans & corn: Heat 3 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add 4 pieces garlic cloves, minced, 2 teaspoons cumin, and 1 teaspoons chili powder, stir 5 minutes, then add 3 pieces cans black beans (15 oz each), drained & rinsed and 2 cups frozen corn. Cook until corn is lightly charred, about 5 minutes.

Add tomatoes & lime: Stir in 1 pieces can diced tomatoes, drained and cook 2 more minutes. Season with 1 teaspoons salt and squeeze in 2 pieces limes, juiced.

Assemble: Divide quinoa into bowls and top with the bean mixture. Add 1 cups shredded cheddar or crumbled feta (optional) if using.

#### Turmeric Rice & Chickpea Bowl

- 2.5 cups long-grain white rice (dry)
- 3 pieces cans chickpeas (15 oz each), drained & rinsed
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 pieces large yellow onion, diced
- 5 pieces garlic cloves, minced
- 2 teaspoons turmeric
- 1 teaspoons cumin
- 1 teaspoons smoked paprika
- 1 pieces can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz), drained
- 1 teaspoons salt
- 1 pieces lemon, juiced
- 1 cups plain Greek yogurt or tahini (to serve)

Cook turmeric rice: Cook 2.5 cups long-grain white rice (dry) with a pinch of salt and 1 tsp of 2 teaspoons turmeric stirred into the cooking water. This turns the rice golden and fragrant.

Crisp the chickpeas: Pat 3 pieces cans chickpeas (15 oz each), drained & rinsed very dry with a paper towel. Heat 2 tbsp of 4 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chickpeas and cook undisturbed for 6 minutes, then stir and cook another 3 minutes until golden and slightly crispy. Season with half of 1 teaspoons smoked paprika and a pinch of 1 teaspoons salt. Remove and set aside.

Build the sauce: In the same pan, heat remaining 4 tablespoons olive oil over medium heat. Add 1 pieces large yellow onion, diced and cook until soft, about 10 minutes. Add 5 pieces garlic cloves, minced, 1 teaspoons cumin, remaining 2 teaspoons turmeric and 1 teaspoons smoked paprika, and stir 1 minute. Add 1 pieces can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz), drained and cook until thickened, about 4 minutes.

Finish & assemble: Return chickpeas to the pan and toss to coat. Season with 1 teaspoons salt and squeeze in 1 pieces lemon, juiced. Serve over rice with a dollop of 1 cups plain Greek yogurt or tahini (to serve).
