Yields ~4.5 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup (200g) dried soybeans
- 1 Tbsp sugar
- 3 1/2 cups (840g) water

## Preparation

- Clean dried soy beans and soak in water for 4 to 8 hours
- Steam for about 15 minutes. Drain and let cool.
- Add 1Â½ cups (260 g) cooked beans, sugar, and water into the blender in the order listed. Slowly increase to its highest speed and blend for 45 seconds.
- Optionally, strain the milk through a filtration bag or pass through a fine-mesh sieve
