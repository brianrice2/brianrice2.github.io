title = "Crispy Spiced Chickpeas"
+++

## Ingredients

- 2 cans or 1 cup dry chickpeas (cooked), rinsed and dried
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper

## Preparation

Saute the chickpeas in plenty of oil over high heat until nicely golden and crisped (around 5-8 for freshly cooked beans or 12-15 for canned).

While frying, mix the spices in a bowl. Then toss the chickpeas in the bowl.
