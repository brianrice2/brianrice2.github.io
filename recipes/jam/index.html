Refrigerator Jam

 
Table of Contents
  • Ingredients
  • Preparation
 

As simple as it gets.

Total time: ~30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fruit, chopped
  • 1/2 to 2/3 cup (100-130 g) granulated sugar
  • 3 Tbsp lemon juice (approx. 1 Aldi lemon fully squeezed)

Preparation

  1. Place a plate in the freezer for testing the doneness later
  2. Combine the ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat and mash with a fork or potato masher (alternatively, chop the fruit finely using a blender or food processor)
  3. Stirring frequently, reduce until thickened, about 15-20 minutes
  4. To check for doneness, drop a small amount of jam onto a small frozen plate. Let cool for a moment, then run a spoon or finger through the jam. If you can draw a line with clean edges that don’t drip, then it’s ready. If it runs back together, then cook for a few more minutes and try again.

Enjoy!
