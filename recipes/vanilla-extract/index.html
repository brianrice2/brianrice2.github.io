Vanilla Extract

 
Table of Contents
  • Ingredients
  • Preparation
  • Variations
 

Ingredients

  • 1 oz (5-6 count) vanilla beans, cut lengthwise
  • 1 cup vodka, 80-100 proof

Preparation

Combine in a jar. Seal and store in a cool, dark place for 3-6 months, shaking the jar every few weeks.

Variations

  • Try bourbon or another alcohol instead of the traditional vodka
