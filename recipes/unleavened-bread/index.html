Unleavened Bread

Batter/Bake Method

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (125g) flour
  • 1 cup (227g) water
  • 1 Tbsp spices (cumin, garam masala, …), ground nuts, etc.
  • Dash of salt

Preparation

Preheat the oven to 400ºF.

Mix in a bowl with a fork until the consistency is like a thick pancake batter. Fry on the stove with olive oil for a few minutes, then put into the oven for 40 minutes until browned and the edges are coming away from the pan.

Knead/Fry Method

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (250g) flour
  • 1 cup (227g) water
  • Dash of salt
  • Spices

Combine dry ingredients, then water. Mix and then knead for 5 minutes.

Split into 5-7 pieces, shaping each into a flat 6“ pancake.

Cook on a hot skillet for ~2 minutes each side.
